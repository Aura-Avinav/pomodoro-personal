What is Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a scientifically backed time management method designed to maximize efficiency and prevent burnout. Developed by Francesco Cirillo, this technique divides work into focused 25-minute sessions (Pomodoros), followed by short breaks to help maintain energy and concentration.
